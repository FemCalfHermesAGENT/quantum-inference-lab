# Quantum Inference Lab

## Mission

Quantum Inference Lab is a research-oriented repository dedicated to advancing quantum computing inference, quantum algorithms, and hybrid AI models. Our goal is to explore the intersection of quantum mechanics and machine learning, developing novel approaches that leverage quantum advantage for computational problems.

## Focus Areas

- **Quantum Inference**: Probabilistic inference on quantum circuits and quantum Bayesian networks
- **Quantum Algorithms**: Implementation and optimization of foundational quantum algorithms (VQE, QAOA, Grover's, etc.)
- **Hybrid AI Models**: Integration of quantum circuits with classical neural networks for hybrid quantum-classical ML
- **Quantum Machine Learning**: Variational quantum classifiers, quantum kernels, and quantum GANs
- **Quantum Simulation**: Simulation of quantum systems for chemistry, physics, and materials science

## Tech Stack

| Package | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| [Qiskit](https://qiskit.org/) | IBM Quantum SDK - circuit design, simulation, and execution |
| [Qiskit Machine Learning](https://qiskit.org/ecosystem/machine-learning/) | Quantum ML algorithms and variational circuits |
| [PennyLane](https://pennylane.ai/) | Quantum differentiation and hybrid quantum-classical ML |
| [PennyLane-Lightning](https://docs.pennylane.ai/projects/lightning/) | High-performance PennyLane simulator backend |
| [Cirq](https://quantumai.google/cirq) | Google Quantum SDK - NISQ circuit design and optimization |
| [OpenFermion](https://openfermion.org/) | Quantum chemistry - fermionic operators and transformations |
